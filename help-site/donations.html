Thank you!

Thank you for your willingness to contribute to our archive. Your donation will mean that community members can access information about their country, history and ancestors.

How it works

To contribute material to the archive, you describe it in a spreadsheet (or set of spreadsheets) that sit alongside your files. To make this easier, we have prepared two starter packs. You can discuss with us which of these packs best fits your collection. Download the one that fits your collection, unzip it, and start filling in the spreadsheets.

Not sure how to fill them in? See the Archive Spreadsheet Guide for what every column means.

Simple pack

Best for smaller donations. One spreadsheet for your items, plus a folder to drop your files into.

  • metadata.xlsx — describe your items
  • files/ — put your files here
Download simple pack (.zip)

Full pack

Best for large or complex collections. Includes extra spreadsheets for people, places, organisations, languages and licenses.

  • metadata.xlsx — describe your items
  • files/ — put your files here
  • People, Organisations, Places, Localities
  • Languages, Licenses
Download full pack (.zip)

Only need the basics? Choose the simple pack — you do not have to use every spreadsheet, and we can help fill in the rest.

The download packs are generated automatically from the archive tool, so they always match what the app itself creates.
