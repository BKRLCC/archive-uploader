About Balachi

Balachi is the app used to catalogue and contribute material to a community archive. It helps you describe items — photos, recordings, documents and objects — and the people, places and organisations they relate to, so they can be preserved and shared.

Use the links below to download the app, learn how to fill in the spreadsheets, or contribute your own collection.
