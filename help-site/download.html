Installing on a Mac

  1. Double click on the downloaded .zip file to open it.
  2. Drag the app into your Applications folder.
  3. Click on the app to open it.

The first time you open it, macOS may ask you to confirm you want to open an app downloaded from the internet — click Open.

Installing on Windows

  1. Double click on the downloaded .exe file to run it.
  2. If Windows shows a “Windows protected your PC” message, click More info, then Run anyway.
  3. The app installs automatically and opens when it's finished. After that, you can find it in your Start menu.
